Collaborators

Current Rockstars

Joy Chen

Joy is a third year Business Administration and Statistics double major. Joy joined the team during her freshman year and has been involved with all different aspects of research since then including experimental design and data analysis. Joy intends to pursue a career in the financial industry and plans on attending graduate school in the future. During her free time, Joy enjoys equestrian horseback riding, camping, and trying new cuisines.

Ashley Nguyen

Ashley Nguyen is a third year student, pursuing degrees in Business Administration and Psychology at UC Berkeley. She joined the research team in Spring 2013 after realizing her fascination with the subjects of organizational behavior and social psychology. In the near future, she plans on becoming an accountant; however, her ultimate goal is to, one day, own a wedding planning business. Outside of research, Ashley enjoys exploring new restaurants, and spending the rest of her free time shopping in San Francisco.

Collin Gallagher

Collin is a third year Business Administration and Economics double major. Professionally, Collin is interested in pursuing a career in investment management or investment banking. Collin's research interests include the effects of different influence styles on company cultures as well as research into the effects of power on group dynamics. Outside of school and research, Collin works part-time at a hedge fund in San Francisco. Originally from Orange County, Collin enjoys surfing, baseball, country music, and U.S. history.

Andrew Fang

Andrew is a third year Electrical Engineering & Computer Science and Applied Math double major. He is interested in both front-end and mobile development, and looks to exercise the skills has learned as a software engineer in the Silicon Valley. He also hopes to move into management, later on in his career, and is considering an MBA. New to this research lab, Andrew hopes to learn about and contribute to the study of social and organizational behavior. Andrew loves to relax on the beach, play basketball, and go camping.
