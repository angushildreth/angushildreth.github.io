Overview

We are a group of undergraduate students and a graduate student who work on social psychological and organizational behavior research. We work with faculty at UC Berkeley’s Haas School of Business and at other US business schools.

Our lab has a strong culture of learning and collaboration. Members get involved in all aspects of the research process and are expected to seek new challenges and continue to grow.

Since 2009, more than 60 undergraduate researchers have been part of the lab. Profiles of our current lab members and some of our illustrious alumnae are set out below.

How to apply

We hire new researchers every semester through Berkeley’s Undergraduate Research Apprentice Program. Click here to find out more.
