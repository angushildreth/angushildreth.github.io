Angus is a doctoral candidate studying organizational behavior at the University of California, Berkeley’s Haas School of Business.

He graduated from the University of Cambridge with a Master's degree in mathematics and has an MBA from the University of California, Berkeley. He is a Fellow of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries and worked for more than a decade as a consulting actuary for Aon and PricewaterhouseCoopers. He spent two years managing the affairs of PricewaterhouseCoopers’ Global CEO and Global Leadership Team before pursuing his PhD.

His research examines the dysfunctionality of groups and focuses on the effects of power and loyalty on group members’ collaborative and ethical behavior.
