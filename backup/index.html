Collaborators

Current Rockstars

Joy Chen

Joy is a third year Business Administration and Statistics double major at UC Berkeley. She intends to pursue a career in the financial industry and plans to attend graduate school in the future. Joy joined the team during her freshman year in Spring 2012 and has worked on research for Professors Anderson, Nelson, Chatman and Bazerman. She has been involved with many different aspects of research since joining the team from experimental design to complex analyses. During her free time, Joy enjoys equestrian horseback riding, camping, and trying new cuisines.

Tommy Shi

Tommy is a third year student studying Business Administration and Pre-Medicine. He is interested in a career in medicine and business, and aspires to eventually become a physician. Tommy joined the team in his freshman year in Spring 2012. He has worked on various research projects relating to power, ethics and the experience of emotions for a number of faculty at Haas. Outside of academia, Tommy loves to cook, read, eat, and play basketball.

Arushi Saxena

Arushi Saxena is a third year studying Business Administration and Human-Centered Design. Arushi joined the team in her freshman year in Spring 2012, and she appreciates that it compels her to ask relevant questions that often remain un-asked (Think: Why do powerful people choose to sit at the head of the table rather than the middle?). In the future, Arushi aspires to combine her interests in enterprise finance, technology, and international development. For fun, Arushi enjoys reading about gastronomy and exploring the Bay Area through mountain hiking.

Collin Gallagher

Collin is a third year Business Administration and Economics double major at UC Berkeley. He intends pursuing a career in investment management or investment banking and is currently working part-time at a hedge fund in San Francisco. Collin joined the research team in his sophomore year in Fall 2012 and has worked on research for Professors Anderson and Chatman. His research interests relate to the effects of individual and group power on group processes and organizational culture. He has helped run experiments, analyze data in excel and SPSS an is now training a new cohort of researchers on online survey tools. Originally from Orange County, Collin enjoys surfing, baseball, country music, and U.S. history.

Ashley Nguyen

Ashley is a third year student, pursuing degrees in Business Administration and Psychology at UC Berkeley. She joined the research team in her sophomore year in Spring 2013 after realizing her fascination with organizational behavior and social psychology. Ashley has run a number of studies relating to ethical behavior in the research lab for professor Bazerman. In the near future, she plans to become an accountant, though her ultimate goal is to own a wedding planning business. Outside of research, Ashley enjoys exploring new restaurants, and spending the rest of her free time shopping in San Francisco.

Neel Patel

Neel is an Economics major in his 2nd year at Berkeley. He intends to pursue a career in the finance industry after he graduates as well as gain masters degrees in finance and business administration. His ultimate goal is to start his own company. Neel joined the lab in his sophomore year in Fall 2013 and is interested in the way organizational behavior can be quantified in a business context. He hopes to learn the necessary quantitative skills to thrive in the financial industry after he graduates. Raised in India and partially educated in England, Neel enjoys travelling to different countries, practicing Karate, cooking seafood, and playing squash.

Andrew Fang

Andrew is a third year Electrical Engineering & Computer Science and Applied Math double major. He is interested in both front-end and mobile development, and looks to exercise the skills he has learned as a software engineer in Silicon Valley. He also hopes to move into management, later on in his career, and is considering an MBA. Andrew joined the lab in his Junior year in Fall 2013 and hopes to learn about and contribute to the study of social psychology and organizational behavior. Andrew loves to relax on the beach, play basketball, and go camping.
