Angus is a doctoral candidate studying organizational behavior at the University of California, Berkeley’s Haas School of Business.

He graduated from the University of Cambridge with a Master's degree in mathematics and has an MBA from the University of California, Berkeley. He is a Fellow of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries and worked for more than a decade as a consulting actuary for Aon and PricewaterhouseCoopers (PwC). He spent two years managing the affairs of PwC’s Global CEO and Global Leadership Team before pursuing his PhD.

My research examines how power and loyalty – two primary dimensions of social relations - affect cognition, behavior, and performance at the individual and group levels.
